Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 28, 2026

1. Introduction & Data Controller

Tuxedo Cat Apps ("we," "our," or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share information about you when you use the Alarm Rotation mobile application (the "App").

For the purposes of the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and the Brazilian General Data Protection Law (LGPD), Tuxedo Cat Apps is the Data Controller responsible for your personal data. By using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

2. Information We Collect

We collect data to provide, secure, and improve our services. We strictly adhere to data minimization principles.

  • Locally Stored Data: The App saves your alarm times, custom song selections, downloaded AI audio briefings (MP3s), and appearance preferences locally on your device. We do not transmit or store this configuration data on our servers.
  • Location Data: If you enable the weather feature for your AI alarm briefing, we collect your approximate or precise location coordinates at the time the alarm triggers.
  • Subscription & Billing Data: If you purchase the Premium AI Briefing, we collect anonymous purchase tokens from the Google Play Store to verify your active subscription status.
  • Automatically Collected Data: The App and its third-party services may collect diagnostic data, including your IP address, device name, OS version, and crash logs.
  • Diagnostic & Debug Logs: The App may transmit debug logs (device info, App state) to our servers to help us troubleshoot technical issues.
  • Diagnostic Identifier: You are assigned an anonymous, randomly generated Diagnostic ID via Firebase Authentication, used solely to associate logs with your device instance.

3. How We Use Your Data & Our Legal Basis

  • Deliver AI Audio Briefing: Performance of a Contract.
  • Provide Weather Updates: Consent (revocable in Android settings).
  • Troubleshoot & Improve Stability: Legitimate Interest.
  • Verify Subscriptions: Performance of a Contract / Legal Obligation.
  • Display Advertisements: Consent / Legitimate Interest.

4. Third-Party Services and Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. Your data is securely processed by:

  • Google Cloud & Firebase: Secure backend, authentication, and bug reporting.
  • Google Generative AI (Gemini) & Cloud Text-to-Speech: Processes text scripts into audio.
  • Open-Meteo API: Returns weather forecasts based on your coordinates.
  • Google Play Developer API: Processes subscription tokens.
  • Google AdMob: Serves ads using advertising identifiers.

5. International Data Transfers

By using the App, your data may be transferred to and processed in the United States or other countries. We ensure compliance with GDPR and LGPD via recognized legal safeguards, such as Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs).

6. Data Retention

Our cloud backend is stateless. Real-time data (like location or news topics) is processed ephemerally. Debug logs and bug reports are stored securely for up to 90 days before permanent deletion.

7. Your Privacy Rights (GDPR & LGPD)

You have the right to access, rectify, erase, or request a copy of your data. Because we do not collect names or emails, you must provide your Diagnostic ID (found in the App's 'Info' screen) for us to identify server-side data.

8. Data Security

Local data security is managed by your device's native features (lock screen/biometrics). All cloud transmissions are encrypted using HTTPS.

9. Children's Privacy

The App does not address anyone under the age of 13 (or 16 in certain jurisdictions). We do not knowingly collect information from children.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this policy periodically. Changes are effective immediately upon posting and updating the "Effective Date" at the top of this document.

11. Contact Us & Data Protection Officer

For questions or to exercise your data rights, contact our Data Protection Officer at: privacy@alarmrotation.com.
